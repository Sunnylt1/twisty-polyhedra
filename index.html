  • To rotate the puzzle, press (right mouse button or ctrl key) & drag the mouse.

    Every puzzle has an implicit axis of rotation. Mouse movement along the horizontal direction will cause the puzzle to rotate along this axis. Mouse movement along the vertical direction will cause the puzzle to rotate along the left-right axis.

  • To twist a slice, move the cursor to a sticker, press (left mouse button or alt key) & drag the mouse.

    Note that for easier handling, It is recommended to keep one of the faces close to the front & use it's stickers to twist slices.

  • Select a puzzle from menu & Trigger automatic scramble.

Happy Solving!
